About my school

Fravashi International Academy has been awarded the 13th position in India, the 3rd position in Maharashtra and the 1st position in Nashik in the year 2019-2020 in the India school rankings by the ‘Education World’. The academic reputation, faculty competence, leadership quality, sports education and interaction with parents, educationists, principals and teachers around the country were the key parameters used to decide the list of fifteen top ranking schools in India.


The school has been made by Mr. Ratan Luth who is the Chairman, R. S. Luth Education Trust. He said that "I have always believed that dreams turn into reality only by hard work, as Donovan Bailey has rightly said, “Follow your passion, be prepared to work hard, sacrifice and, above all, don’t let anyone limit your dreams.” An institution is the culmination of the power of a dream and a team. Keeping in mind the need to develop and train children who would be the future global citizens, I decided to introduce to Nashik its very first International day cum boarding school, Fravashi International Academy (FIA). I believe our appeal as a school rests in our strong sense of community, the values and discipline we inculcate, our commitment to realizing the potential of young students, and providing a balanced education that addresses the academic, social, emotional, physical and spiritual aspects of our students’ lives."

Events

Fravashi International Academy organizes numerous events, which give a platform to the students to portray their talent. There are various events like the MUN – Model United Nations, Annual day, Sports day, celebration of the colours Blue, Red, Green, Yellow and Yoga Day. Secularism day is celebrated each year on Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru’s birthday and Girls Day is celebrated each year on Woman’s Day. In addition, the pre-primary section celebrates all the festivals with joy and fervour. At, FIA, a grand celebration is organized on Independence Day and Republic day too. Events like Christmas party, farewell party, bring out comfort and fun within the students. These events are planned and implemented with a view to bring out the best in the child.


Model United Nations, also known as MUN, is an event activity in which students typically role-play delegates of the United Nations and simulate UN committees. This activity takes place at MUN conferences, which is usually organized by the MUN delegates from various schools. These activities build the participants’ self-confidence to open up and speak without hesitation. Students participate at inter-school conferences and get an opportunity to meet other students participating in the conference coming from around the country and the world.
